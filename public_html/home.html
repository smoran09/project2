About

Welcome to History of the Internet. The material briefly focuses on the early invention of the internet, the mechanics behind the production, and it's affect on society through economic, social, and legal means.

The years observed span from the 1950's until the early 2000's. The content displayed details a history rich in success through a series of roles. From inventor, creator, and coder to users like, us.
